Effective date: 2026-04-17

1. Overview

MicroEscape is designed to work offline and minimize data by default. We do not require an account to use the app.

2. Data We Collect

  • We do not collect personal data by default.
  • We do not track you across apps or websites.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

MicroEscape may store the following locally on your device:

  • App settings (e.g., language, filters, session duration).
  • Optional content packs cache (only if you enable content updates).
  • Subscription status cache (where applicable; used only to reflect Pro access in the app).

We do not store usage history (e.g., which cards you viewed) as a feature.

4. Network Access

  • The app can be used offline.
  • Optional content updates are disabled by default and can be limited to Wi‑Fi only when enabled.
  • If you enable AI-based content refill (if available), the app will connect to generate new content packs and then store them locally for offline use. I do not send sensitive personal information for this feature.

5. In‑App Purchases (Subscriptions)

If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Apple (App Store). MicroEscape uses Apple’s purchase/entitlement information to unlock Pro features and to restore purchases. I do not receive your full payment details.

6. Sharing

MicroEscape does not share personal data with third parties for advertising. MicroEscape does not sell personal information.

7. Children

MicroEscape is not intended for children under 13.

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available at this URL.
